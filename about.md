I'm Abin Rimal, a cybersecurity graduate and software engineer based in Sydney, Australia.

I completed a Master of Information Technology with a Cybersecurity specialisation at CIHE Australia in 2026, following seven years of professional software engineering at Prabidhi Labs in Kathmandu — where I managed security and infrastructure for 100+ production web environments across AWS, Linux, Shopify, and WordPress.

My capstone research project was a Hybrid Honeypot System: a multi-sensor platform integrating Cowrie, Glastopf, Snort IDS, and a machine learning anomaly detector, designed to capture and analyse attacker behaviour in realistic simulated environments.

I hold an ACS Skills Assessment — independent verification of my overseas technical qualifications for the Australian market — and full working rights in Australia under Graduate Visa Subclass 485.

Currently: ICT Security & Systems Support at Warrimoo Kitchens | Freelance Shopify Developer | Volunteer Web Developer at Australian Refugee Volunteers Inc.

I write here about cybersecurity, software engineering, DevOps, and navigating the Australian tech industry as an internationally trained developer.
